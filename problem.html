THE PROBLEM


Problem Statement


Anxiety and depression can drain the motivation of an individual, causing them to fail to complete their daily activities, and leading to further demotivation.


Problem Characteristics

  • Most suggested solutions to mental health requires money and meeting with a professional, a resource most teens and young adults lack.
  • Teens and adults who have busy schedules may struggle to stay organized and complete the necessary day to day tasks.
  • Individuals often sacrifice well-being and self-care in order to complete work related and school related responsibilities. A continuous lack of self-care can lead to stronger symptoms of depression.
  • For those who struggle to do anything at all, figuring out where to start can be overwhelming.
